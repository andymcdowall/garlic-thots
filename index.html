Our story

THE ESTABLISHMENT

✦ ✦ ✦

Family-owned since 1987, Garlic Thots has been Chicagoland's worst-kept secret — and its most beloved destination. Pizza, sure. But we put on a SHOW.

Every pie is hand-tossed by our world-class performers, or pizzaiolos. Dough spun high in the air, fingers working the dough with a precision that will either make you wish you were them or wish you had them.

Our founder, Sal "Silky" Genovese, opened Garlic Thots after a life-changing trip to Naples where he witnessed a master pizzaiolo full-knuckle a ball of dough into something transcendent. "I want people to feel what I felt," Sal told the Chicago Tribune in 1989. "A little hot, a little hungry, and completely unable to look away."

Forty-six years later, that remains the mission statement.
